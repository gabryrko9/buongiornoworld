Our mission

We promote one simple, radical habit: starting every day with human connection instead of headlines and feeds.

A good morning seems like a small thing — until you notice how many people never receive one. People who live alone. People far from home. People going through a hard season. Loneliness is one of the quietest struggles of our time, and it begins first thing in the morning, in the silence of a phone full of news and empty of voices.

Buongiorno, World exists to change what the first minute of your day feels like. Instead of an algorithm, a person. Instead of outrage, warmth. Instead of content, a window: forty seconds of a real voice — a grandmother in Sicily, a fisherman in Japan, a teacher in Nairobi — telling you something true from their life, and wishing you well.

We believe positivity isn't naive — it's a practice. And like every practice, it works best when it's daily, human, and shared.

What we promote

  • Human connection across borders, languages and generations — one real voice at a time.
  • Daily positivity as a morning ritual, not an empty slogan: a concrete, warm start to every day.
  • Cultural exchange — every video is a window onto a real place and a real way of living.
  • The fight against loneliness — so that nobody, anywhere, starts their day unseen.
  • Slow, intentional media — one video a day. No feed. No scroll. No ads. Ever.

Questions

Everything you might wonder.

And if you wonder something else, write to us — a human answers.

How do I receive my daily good morning?
You choose your wake-up time and your channel: push notification, WhatsApp/Telegram message, or email. Every morning, one video arrives — and that's it. There is no feed to open, nothing else to watch, no "you might also like". One window a day, then the project gets out of your way.
Who are the people in the videos?
Real people from all over the world who sent us their good morning: grandmothers, fishermen, teachers, bakers, students. Every video is reviewed by hand, subtitled in English, and published with the person's consent, their first name, their place, and a short story about who they are. No influencers, no actors, no AI-generated people — ever.
Why isn't everything free?
Because nothing on the internet is truly free — most "free" products are paid for with your attention and your data. We'd rather be paid in coffee money and answer only to you. The subscription (from €2/month) pays for curation, subtitles, and delivery, and keeps the project completely free of ads and algorithms. And one morning a week — Sunday — is free for everyone, forever.
Can I gift it to someone?
Yes, and we think it's the best way to use it. You can gift packs of 5, 30, or 365 mornings for birthdays and holidays — or earn 5 gift mornings by contributing your own good morning video. The recipient just gets a link, picks their wake-up time, and the mornings begin.
Do the people in the videos get anything?
Every published contributor gets free access to the daily mornings and 5 gift mornings to share. As the project grows, our goal is to give back more to the people who make it possible — they are the project.
What languages are the videos in?
All of them — that's the point. Sicilian, Japanese, Swahili, Spanish, and whatever arrives next. Every video keeps its original voice and is subtitled in English, so the warmth always crosses the border.
Can I choose where my mornings come from?
By default you get the full journey — a different corner of the world each day. Subscribers can also send window requests ("I'd love a morning from Vietnam") and we do our best to open that window.

We believe every person on Earth carries a morning like that: shaped by a place, a history, a life. This site exists to collect them — one window at a time — so that every day, someone somewhere wakes up a little less alone.
