What we believe

A good morning seems like a small thing — until you notice how many people never receive one.

People who live alone. People far from home. People going through a hard season. This project exists for one simple reason: nobody should start their day unseen. And positivity, like a good morning, only works when it's passed on.

🤝

Connection over content

This is not a feed and there is nothing to scroll. One person, once a day, looking into a camera and meaning it. The opposite of social media: a moment made for you, not for likes.

☀️

Positivity is passed on

A good morning is the smallest unit of kindness that exists. It costs nothing, takes seconds, and changes the temperature of a whole day. We believe it travels: receive one, and you'll want to give one.

🪟

Every life is a window

Behind every greeting there's a farm in Sicily, a fishing boat in Japan, a classroom in Nairobi. Forty seconds of a real voice can teach you more about a place than an hour of television.

How it works

One person. One story. One window.

Simple by design — because mornings should be.

STEP 01

Someone shares their morning

In their own language, from their kitchen, boat, or balcony. Not just "good morning" — something only they could say, shaped by their land and their life.

STEP 02

We subtitle it in English

Every video keeps its original voice. Subtitles make sure the warmth crosses every border it meets.

STEP 03

You receive one each morning

Delivered when you wake up. Before the news, before the inbox — a human being, wishing you well.

The morning exchange

Give one morning.
Get five back.

Positivity shouldn't be consumed — it should circulate. So this is how it works here: send us your good morning video, and you unlock 5 days of the Daily Good Morning — one video delivered to you every morning, free.

  • Your window joins the collection, with your name, your place, and your story.
  • For 5 mornings, you wake up to someone else's good morning from another corner of Earth.
  • Want more mornings? Send another window — or invite someone whose good morning the world should hear.

The project is free and always will be. The exchange isn't a paywall — it's the engine: every morning received here was given by someone first.

Why this exists

"
Cucina sempri quarchi cosa pi cu' ti sta vicinu.

Always cook something for the people around you.

This whole project began with one voice: my grandmother's, in Ragusa, Sicily. Every morning she says good morning the same way — and in those few seconds there is her farm, her years as an emigrant in New York, her six grandchildren, her kitchen that is never closed to anyone. A whole way of caring for people, compressed into a greeting.

We believe every person on Earth carries a morning like that: shaped by a place, a history, a life. This site exists to collect them — one window at a time — so that every day, someone somewhere wakes up a little less alone.
